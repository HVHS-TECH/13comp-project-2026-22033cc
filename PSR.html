Paper Scissors Rock Game

Paper Scissors Rock-like game is a 2 player multi player game. Two players on two seperate devices go head to head in a thrilling paper scisors rock game. by correctly guessing the one that beats your opponents one, you win a point.
First to three wins!

How to play Paper Scissors Rock-like Game

If by some miracle you don't know how to play Paper Scissors Rock, here is how to play: When a round starts, you choose one out of the three options:
    • Paper
      Scissors
      Rock
    • Your opponent will also choose one out of these three. each one beats another, and loses against another.
    • Paper beats rock
      Scissors cuts Paper
      Rock crushes scissors
    • if you choose the same, its a tie. Otherwise, whoever wins based on these wins!
